Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions we receive about our beef.

What is grass-fed beef?

Grass-fed beef comes from cattle that have been raised on a diet of grass and
forage. This is in contrast to conventional beef, which is typically raised on a
diet of grains and soy. Grass-fed beef is often considered to be more nutritious
and environmentally friendly than conventional beef.

How is it raised?

Our cattle are raised on pasture and rotated to fresh grass daily. We do not use
antibiotics or hormones, and our cattle are never fed grain or soy. We practice
regenerative farming techniques to improve soil health and sequester carbon.

How do I refer?

Our beef is available for purchase online, and we offer delivery to your door. We
also offer pickup at our ranch in Texas. You can order whole or half cows, as well as
individual cuts of beef.

How do I cook it?

Grass-fed beef cooks differently than conventional beef due to its lower fat
content. We recommend cooking it at a lower temperature and for a shorter time to
prevent it from drying out. You can also marinate it to add moisture and flavour.

How do I store it?

Grass-fed beef should be stored in the refrigerator or freezer to maintain its
freshness. We recommend storing it in the coldest part of your refrigerator or in the
freezer for long-term storage. You can also vacuum-seal it to prevent freezer burn.

How do I order?

Ordering our beef is easy! Simply visit our website and select the cuts of beef you
would like to purchase. You can choose from whole or half cows, as well as individual
cuts. We offer delivery to your door or pickup at our ranch in Texas.
